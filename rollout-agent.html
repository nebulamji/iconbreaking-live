The 11-Week Protocol

Three phases — Pre-Release, Release, Post-Release. Check off each week's deliverables to earn recognition. Click a week to expand.

Music Release Metadata Standard

The metadata layer Julian & Aggie flagged as the foundation. Get this right and every DSP, playlist pitch, and royalty stream is clean.

Core Identifiers

ISRC per track · UPC per release · explicit flag · release date · label & P-line · original release date.

Credits & Splits

Writers, composers, publishers, performers, producers — with publishing splits % for each. Missing splits = delayed or misrouted royalties.

DSP Fields

Spotify Canvas (3–8s), Apple lyrics & songwriter credits, YouTube Music Art Tracks, Amazon HD flag, TikTok sound start date.

Assets

3000×3000 cover (RGB, no upscaled), 16:9 video where possible, clean instrumental & acapella stems for remix/UGC.

Delivery

Distributor upload ≥ 3 weeks before release. Locked metadata no later than T-14 days. Pitching windows close before street date.

Rights & Clearance

Sample clearances, feature agreements, master use licenses — in writing before distribution. Aggie logs each in the rollout.
