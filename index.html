The Aggie stack

Six modules. One agent.

Aggie is not a chatbot. It's an operating system for artist services. Each module runs on real signals — audience data, content performance, release windows, and industry pattern-recognition.

01 · Release Readiness Engine

Score your window before you spend.

Aggie analyzes audience signals, content momentum, and release timing to tell you if you're ready — or what's missing. No more launching into silence.

02 · Audience Intelligence

Know who's listening before they know you.

AI-powered audience mapping: who follows, who engages, who converts, who's about to churn. Real signals, not vanity metrics.

03 · Content System

The calendar builds itself.

Feed the agent a call, a meeting, or a pain point. It generates the content calendar, writes the copy, and schedules the posts. You review and approve.

04 · Agent Fulfillment

24/7 operations. No sleep required.

The agent answers leads, follows up on DMs, sends the right content at the right time, and flags the signals that need a human. Faraji's fulfillment pain becomes your operating system.

05 · Campaign Infrastructure

Meta, Spotify, and everything between.

Operational setup that kills the bottleneck: Meta Business Suite, Spotify for Artists, ad campaigns, and distribution. The unglamorous work that unlocks everything.

06 · Music Challenge Loop

Artists market each other.

City Limits challenge, cover contests, remix loops. Artists tag themselves, create social proof, and grow each other's audiences. The challenge is the demand engine.
