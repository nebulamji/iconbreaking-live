The window

Why 6–8 weeks?

Because this is the last realistic window to build the campaign before the release becomes reactive. At 6–8 weeks out, you still have time to fix content, capture demand, organize the team, and create enough signal for fans, managers, playlist editors, and industry people to understand why the drop matters.

The problem

The song is not the rollout.

Creative / Artist

  • You have the song, but not the campaign.
  • You're posting, but not building demand.
  • You're scared your release will disappear in 48 hours.
  • You don't know what to do each week before launch.

Manager / Team

  • The artist or project has momentum, but nobody owns the rollout.
  • No one is tracking content, fan capture, outreach, or follow-up.
  • The campaign depends on memory, texts, and vibes.
  • You can't tell what signal is real.

A&R / Producer

  • The music is strong, but the campaign is not legible.
  • No clean story, launch board, signal, or proof.
  • You need to know if this project can actually move.
  • Comments and DMs happen, but nobody captures, prioritizes, or follows up.
